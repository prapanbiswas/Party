Sound Activated Disco Lights — How It Works

LuminaParty's music sync system uses the Web Audio API to capture microphone input, then runs a professional 19-module DSP audio engine. Unlike simple volume-meter lights, this system performs genuine sound activated disco lights analysis — independently measuring bass frequencies (20–250 Hz), mid-range (250–4 kHz), and high frequencies (4–20 kHz) using a 31-band ISO third-octave spectrum analyzer.

Beat Sync Light Browser — Real BPM Tracking

The beat sync light browser system uses onset detection to identify every beat in the music. Detected beats trigger a white flash burst across all connected devices simultaneously. The BPM value is tracked in real time and displayed in the top bar. Hue Rotate mode uses this BPM to spin the color wheel at exactly the tempo of the song — creating a bpm light sync online effect perfectly locked to the music.

Party Lights Sync to Music — No App Needed

All party lights sync to music features work entirely in your browser — Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge. No download, no account, no subscription. Open the LuminaParty party light online controller, go to the Audio tab, and tap Enable Microphone Sync to start your music reactive light online show immediately.
